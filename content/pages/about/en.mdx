## What I do

I help companies get ERP and AI programmes across the line. Most of the work splits three ways:

- **Programme recovery** — projects that are late, over budget, or technically broken. I diagnose what's real vs. what's political, then rebuild the plan.
- **ECC to S/4HANA and cloud ERP** — migration design, cutover, Finance and Supply Chain transformation, data quality.
- **AI on ERP data** — governance, agentic workflows, and practical models that sit on SAP, Oracle, or Microsoft foundations without hallucinating the ledger.

I do not sell licences. I do not resell implementation services. The advice is independent of the vendor.

## My stance on AI in ERP

AI on broken data is worse than no AI. Get the foundation right — clean masters, reconciled ledgers, documented processes — then bring in models. Most of what's sold as "AI transformation" today is model-first. That's backwards.

When I bring AI into a client engagement it starts with data quality and governance, then moves to targeted agentic workflows, then broadens. The order matters.
