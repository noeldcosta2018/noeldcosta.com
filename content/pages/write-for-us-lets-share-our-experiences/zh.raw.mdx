## Submission Guidelines

To ensure quality and relevance, please follow these guidelines:

### Original Content:

Articles must be unique and unpublished elsewhere.

### Word Count:

Posts should range between 1,200 and 2,000 words.

### Formatting:

Use clear headings, bullet points, and short paragraphs to improve readability.

### SEO Optimization:

Include relevant keywords naturally within the content.

### Citations:

Properly [credit any data](https://noeldcosta.com/sap-sd-sales-and-distribution/), images, or quotes used in your article.

### Tone:

Keep it professional yet conversational, [avoiding technical jargon unless necessary](https://noeldcosta.com/how-to-avoid-scope-creep-in-an-sap-implementation/).
