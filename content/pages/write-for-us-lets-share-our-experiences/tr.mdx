## Gönderim Kuralları

To ensure quality and relevance, please follow these guidelines:

### Orijinal İçerik:

Articles must be unique and unpublished elsewhere.

### Sözcük sayısı:

Posts should range between 1,200 and 2,000 words.

### Biçimlendirme:

Use clear headings, bullet points, and short paragraphs to improve readability.

### SEO Optimizasyonu:

Include relevant keywords naturally within the content.

### Alıntılar:

Uygun şekilde [credit any data](https://noeldcosta.com/tr/sap-sd-sales-and-distribution/), images, or quotes used in your article.

### Ton:

Keep it professional yet conversational, [avoiding technical jargon unless necessary](https://noeldcosta.com/tr/how-to-avoid-scope-creep-in-an-sap-implementation/).
