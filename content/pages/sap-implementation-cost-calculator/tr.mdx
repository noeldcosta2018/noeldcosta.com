### 11\. How Much Does an SAP Implementation Cost?

MKS [cost of an SAP](https://noeldcosta.com/tr/sap-conversational-ai-and-successfactors-for-hr-in-2025/) implementation varies widely based on several factors, but here’s a general breakdown to help you understand the range:

##### **Tipik Maliyet Aralığı**

1.  **Küçük işletmeler**: $ 150,000 - $ 500,000

    *   Limited scope with standard modules like Finance, HR, or Procurement.
    *   Minimal customization and fewer users.
2.  **Orta Ölçekli İşletmeler**: 500,000 ila 2 milyon dolar

    *   Broader module implementation, moderate customization, and integration needs.
    *   May include modules like Supply Chain [Management or Customer Experience](https://noeldcosta.com/tr/why-erp-integration-with-salesforce-fails-and-how-to-fix-it/).
3.  **Büyük İşletmeler**: $2 million to $10+ million

    *   Complex, multi-entity deployments with extensive customizations.
    *   Often includes advanced analytics, AI, or IoT integrations and global rollouts.

* * *

##### **Maliyet Bileşenleri**

1.  **Yazılım Lisansları**: Annual licensing fees are based on the number of users and modules.

    *   Small: $30,000–$80,000/year
    *   Mid: $80,000–$200,000/year
    *   Large: $200,000+/year
2.  **[Uygulama Hizmetleri](https://noeldcosta.com/tr/sap-enterprise-warehouse-management-sap-ewm-essentials/)**:

    *   **Danışmanlık ve Geliştirme**: $150–$300/hour (depending on the consultant’s location and expertise).
    *   **Özelleştirme**: Costs increase with the level of system modifications required.
3.  **Hardware/Infrastructure**: Necessary for on-premise implementations, ranging from $50,000 to $250,000+.

4.  **Eğitim ve Değişim Yönetimi**: Often underestimated but critical. This can cost 10–15% of the total budget.

5.  **Veri göçü**: [Cleaning and moving legacy data into SAP](https://noeldcosta.com/tr/sap-clean-core-strategy-what-it-means-for-your-business/) can add significant costs, especially for large datasets or multiple legacy systems.
